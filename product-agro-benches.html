Greenhouse Agro Benches

Ergonomic solutions to optimize space, improve airflow, and enhance labor efficiency in your greenhouse.

Product Overview

Our GI (Galvanized Iron) Benches are designed to organize your growing space efficiently. Raising crops off the ground improves air circulation, drainage, and ease of access for workers, leading to healthier plants and higher productivity.

Key Features

  • Durable Galvanized Iron construction
  • Improved air circulation around plants
  • Better drainage and hygiene
  • Ergonomic working height
