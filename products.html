Explore greenhouse, polyhouse, and net house structures built for productivity, protection, and cost-effective growing environments.

Greenhouse / Poly House

A Greenhouse / Poly House is used to provide a suitable environment for plants. It is economical to run and maintain, reasonably priced, and affordable for most farmers.

Climate Controlled Greenhouse

A Climate Controlled Greenhouse is designed to provide a controlled growing environment for specialty crops and plants.

Key systems typically used include:

  • Special-purpose fans to draw air into the greenhouse
  • High-efficiency cooling pads made of cellulose fibre (high surface area ribbons)
  • Humidity control using an elaborate system of foggers
  • Fertigation through an efficient drip irrigation system

Temperature and humidity can be controlled to provide the exact growing environment required for plants (including those suited to temperate conditions). This type of greenhouse is ideal for growing nursery plants.

Nursery plants are valuable and need a very specific environment and extreme care during early stages.

Net House / Shade House

Net House / Shade House structures are used for sheltering plants that are tender to high-intensity sun heat.

Benefits:

  • Helps ward off birds and rodents that devour seeds and germinating embryos
  • Protects seedlings from desiccating winds
  • Useful for germinating small seeds and handling pricked material that needs special watering

These structures may be fitted with micro sprinklers for fast and uniform irrigation.

Many nursery operations can be performed under shade houses, such as:

  • Seed sowing
  • Pricking
  • Hardening of rooted cuttings

Shade houses also help reduce irrigation frequency and save water. They should be well ventilated; doors and windows should be kept open during working hours so air moves freely.

Structure & Materials

Strong structural engineering paired with durable covering materials for long-lasting installations.

Structure

The structure is made using galvanized iron tubular, SHS, and RHS pipes of different sizes depending on the size and design requirements.

The outer covering may include:

  • Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheet
  • Poly Film
  • Shade Net

The covering is fixed with aluminium profile/section.

Covering Options

We provide three commonly used covering materials:

  • Poly Film: 5-layer UV stabilized film, anti-virus, anti-sulphur, anti-dripping, diffused, imported make, 200 microns thick
  • Shade Net: UV stabilized, available shade densities: 35%, 50%, 75%, 90%
  • Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheet: UV stabilized on both sides, available thickness: 6mm, 8mm, 10mm (and more)

Why We Recommend Polycarbonate Sheets

We generally recommend polycarbonate sheets because, in the long run, they prove economical.

  • The dome design reduces energy consumption to maintain set parameters inside the greenhouse
  • Lightweight
  • More durable and resistant

Light Transmission

  • Clear sheet offers 80% to 84% light transmission for 6mm thickness.
  • 74% to 76% light transmission for thickness above 6mm.
