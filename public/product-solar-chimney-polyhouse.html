Solar Chimney Polyhouse

Advanced passive ventialtion systems harnessing solar thermals for superior climate control.

Product Overview

The Solar Chimney Polyhouse utilizes passive solar heating to create natural updrafts, drawing hot air out of the greenhouse and pulling cooler air in. This innovative design reduces the need for electrical fans, lowering operational costs.

Key Features

  • Energy-efficient natural ventilation
  • Improved temperature regulation
  • Reduced electricity dependency
  • Ideal for research and high-value crops
