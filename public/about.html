About BRK Fabricators

Nearly two decades of trusted experience in protected cultivation solutions.

Our Story

BRK Fabricators, established in 2006, is a Bangalore-based company with nearly two decades of experience in polyhouse, greenhouse, net house, and agricultural shed construction. Since our inception, we have successfully completed numerous projects for farmers, agri-entrepreneurs, nurseries, and institutional clients across Karnataka and nearby regions.

Our strength lies in our practical field experience, skilled workforce, and deep understanding of crop-wise protected cultivation requirements. Over the years, we have earned the trust of our clients by delivering strong structures, honest pricing, timely execution, and dependable after-sales support.

At BRK Fabricators, we believe that a good structure is the backbone of successful farming. Every project we undertake is built with quality materials, standard engineering practices, and a long-term performance mindset.

Our Mission

We are committed to supporting modern agriculture by delivering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective protected cultivation structures. Customer satisfaction, quality workmanship, and long-term relationships are our top priorities.

What We Specialize In

We specialize in the design and construction of Polyhouses, Greenhouses, Net Houses, and Agricultural Sheds using high-quality GI structures and UV-stabilized materials.
