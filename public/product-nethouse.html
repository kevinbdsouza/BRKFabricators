Net House / Shade House

Effective shelter for plants tender to high-intensity sun heat, protecting seedlings from desiccating winds.

Product Overview

Net Houses are essential for lowering the intensity of direct sunlight and reducing water evaporation from the soil. They are particularly useful for seed sowing, pricking, and hardening rooted cuttings.

  • Usage: Nursery operations (seed sowing, pricking, hardening rooted cuttings), shade-loving crops.
  • Materials: UV stabilized shade nets (35%, 50%, 75%, 90% densities).
  • Function: Lowers direct sunlight intensity, reduces soil water evaporation.

Key Benefits

  • Helps ward off birds and rodents that devour seeds.
  • Protects young seedlings from desiccating winds.
  • Allows for fast and uniform irrigation using micro sprinklers.
  • Reduces irrigation frequency and saves water.

Note: Shade houses should be well ventilated; doors and windows should be kept open during working hours to allow free air movement.

Key Features

  • Uniform light diffusion
  • Protection from harsh sun
  • Improved irrigation efficiency
  • Bird and rodent protection
