Structure & Materials

Strong structural engineering paired with durable covering materials for long-lasting installations.

Structure

The structure is made using galvanized iron tubular, SHS, and RHS pipes of different sizes depending on the size and design requirements.

The outer covering may include:

  • Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheet
  • Poly Film
  • Shade Net

The covering is fixed with aluminium profile/section.

Covering Options

We provide three commonly used covering materials:

  • Poly Film: 5-layer UV stabilized film, anti-virus, anti-sulphur, anti-dripping, diffused, imported make, 200 microns thick
  • Shade Net: UV stabilized, available shade densities: 35%, 50%, 75%, 90%
  • Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheet: UV stabilized on both sides, available thickness: 6mm, 8mm, 10mm (and more)

Why We Recommend Polycarbonate Sheets

We generally recommend polycarbonate sheets because, in the long run, they prove economical.

  • The dome design reduces energy consumption to maintain set parameters inside the greenhouse
  • Lightweight
  • More durable and resistant

Light Transmission

  • Clear sheet offers 80% to 84% light transmission for 6mm thickness.
  • 74% to 76% light transmission for thickness above 6mm.

The success of greenhouse investment depends on the quality and quantity of production. To achieve this, it is essential to combine a good structure with an effective climate control system.
