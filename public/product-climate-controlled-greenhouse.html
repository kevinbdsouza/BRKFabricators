Climate Controlled Greenhouse

Designed to provide a precise growing environment for specialty crops, nursery plants, and research.

Product Overview

These advanced structures allow complete control over temperature, humidity, and light. Ideal for high-value crops and nursery plants that demand specific conditions during their early stages.

  • Cooling: High-efficiency fan and pad cooling systems using cellulose fibre (high surface area ribbons).
  • Humidity: Elaborate fogging systems for precise moisture control.
  • Irrigation: Efficient drip fertigation systems for optimal nutrient delivery.
  • Airflow: Special-purpose fans to draw air into the greenhouse.

Temperature and humidity can be strictly controlled to provide the exact growing environment required for plants, including those best suited to temperate conditions. This makes it the ideal solution for growing valuable nursery plants that need specific environments and extreme care during early stages.

Key Features

  • Precise environmental control
  • Year-round cultivation
  • Protection from pests and external weather
  • Ideal for hardening rooted cuttings
