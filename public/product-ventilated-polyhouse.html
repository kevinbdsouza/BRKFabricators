Ventilated Polyhouse

Engineered for maximum natural airflow, preventing heat buildup and ensuring crop vitality.

Product Overview

Ventilated Polyhouses are designed with roof or side vents that allow hot air to escape and fresh air to enter. This constant air exchange helps regulate temperature and humidity, reducing the risk of disease and heat stress for plants.

Key Features

  • Top or side ventilation options
  • Effective temperature reduction
  • Humidity control
  • Suitable for a wide variety of crops
