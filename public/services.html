Fan & Pad Polyhouse (Climate control polyhouse)

The Fan & Pad Polyhouse is an efficient and cost-effective climate control solution designed for hot climatic conditions. It works on the evaporative cooling principle, ensuring uniform temperature reduction and proper air circulation inside the polyhouse.

Cooling pads installed on one side are supplied with water, while heavy-duty exhaust fans on the opposite side draw air through the pads. This process cools the air naturally and maintains a healthy environment for crops.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces internal temperature by 9‚Äì10¬∞C
  • Ensures uniform airflow and humidity control
  • Improves crop growth, yield, and quality
  • Energy-efficient and low maintenance

We provide complete design, supply, and installation of Fan & Pad Polyhouse systems as per site and crop requirements.
