Semi Circle Polyhouse

Aerodynamic design maximizing strength and light distribution for your crops.

Product Overview

The Semi Circle Polyhouse features a curved roof design that offers superior resistance to wind and naturally sheds snow or heavy rain. This geometry also ensures uniform light distribution throughout the day, promoting consistent plant growth.

Key Features

  • Optimal aerodynamics against high winds
  • Excellent light transmission
  • Reduced structural shadow
  • Cost-effective volume-to-surface ratio
